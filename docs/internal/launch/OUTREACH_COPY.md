## General Outreach Rules

| Rule | Reason |
|------|--------|
| **Personalize every message** | Generic outreach gets ignored. Reference their company, community, or content. |
| **Lead with value, not product** | Open with what they can do, not what the server is. |
| **Be honest about maturity** | "Live, validated, 16 tools" — not "revolutionary AI platform." |
| **One CTA per message** | Demo call is the only ask. Do not pitch pricing in outreach. |
| **Follow up once** | One follow-up at 5–7 days. If no response, move on. |
| **No attachment or link spam** | One link maximum (GitHub or docs). No PDFs, decks, or multi-link messages. |
| **Track conversations** | Simple spreadsheet: name, segment, date sent, response, next step. |
