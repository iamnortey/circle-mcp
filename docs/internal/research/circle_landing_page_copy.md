## Landing Page Overview

This is not a full website. It's a 1-3 screen landing page for the Ninobyte Learning Community. The visitor arriving here has likely been warmed up by inortey.com, ninobyte.com, a newsletter, or a direct referral. They don't need to be convinced that AI matters — they need to be convinced that THIS learning environment is worth their time and money.

**The internal question every visitor has:** "Will I actually learn something here, or is this another empty community?"

**The page must answer:** "Yes — here's the structure, here are the programs, here are the people, here's what it costs, and here's how to start."

## COPY PRINCIPLES APPLIED

1. **Reduce anxiety.** The visitor may feel behind. Normalize the starting point. Never imply they should already know something.
2. **Structure over hype.** The biggest differentiator is structure. Programs have clear outcomes, timelines, and deliverables.
3. **Small is a feature.** "Small on purpose. Every member gets attention." More credible than implying scale that doesn't exist.
4. **Connect to Ninobyte.** "Powered by Ninobyte" — leverage whatever brand equity ninobyte.com builds.
5. **Zero "transform" language.** Every instance replaced with "build," "develop," "apply," or "practice."
6. **No guru, no coach.** Isaac is the program designer and teacher, not a personality to follow.
7. **Program-first model.** Community access is a benefit of programs, not a standalone product. Members arrive because they enrolled in something specific.
