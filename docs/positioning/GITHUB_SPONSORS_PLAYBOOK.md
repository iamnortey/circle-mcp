## Sponsorship Tiers

### Recommended Tier Ladder

| Tier | Amount | Frequency | Name | Description |
|------|--------|-----------|------|-------------|
| 1 | $5 | Monthly | **Supporter** | Help keep the tools maintained and documented. You're funding open-source AI infrastructure. |
| 2 | $10 | Monthly | **Operator** | Everything in Supporter, plus your name listed as a sponsor in project docs (optional). |
| 3 | $25 | Monthly | **Builder** | Everything in Operator, plus priority response on GitHub issues and feature input. |
| 4 | $50 | One-time | **Quick Start** | 30-minute async setup review — I'll check your configuration, flag issues, and send recommendations. |
| 5 | $100 | One-time | **Guided Setup** | 1-hour live or async guided setup session. Get Circle MCP running, configured, and tuned for your workflow. |
| 6 | $200 | One-time | **Workflow Design** | Guided Setup plus a custom prompt pack and workflow recommendations tailored to your community structure. |

### Tier Design Rationale

**Tiers 1–3 (Monthly):**
Low-friction recurring support. These are standard open-source sponsorship tiers. Keep descriptions short and honest. Do not overpromise — the value is funding the work itself.

**Tier 4 (One-time, $50):**
Bridges the gap between passive support and active help. Async-only — manageable time commitment. Positions you as someone who offers real operational value, not just code.

**Tier 5 (One-time, $100):**
The core premium tier. Live or async, whichever works. This is the tier that converts operators who want help but don't want to figure it out alone. Scope it clearly: one session, focused on getting them running.

**Tier 6 (One-time, $200):**
For consultants and agencies who want a head start. The custom prompt pack is low marginal effort for you (you've already built the prompt library) but high perceived value for them. Only offer this if you can deliver within a reasonable window.

### What NOT to offer

- Unlimited support at any tier
- SLA-style response time guarantees
- Custom feature development as a sponsor perk
- Recurring high-touch obligations that don't scale
